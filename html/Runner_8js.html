Runner tick

A game loop utility that updates the engine and renderer by one step (a 'tick'). Features delta smoothing, time correction and fixed or dynamic timing. Triggers beforeTick, tick and afterTick events on the engine. Consider just Engine.update(engine, delta) if you're using your own loop. tick
